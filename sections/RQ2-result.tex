Table~\ref{fig:rq2-1} shows that {\tool} has better performance in
detecting CC fixing statements than the two variants (statement-only
and cascading models). This result shows that the {\bf dual-task
learning helps improve FL over the cascading model} (\code{methFL}
$\rightarrow$ \code{stmtFL}). Moreover, without the impact of
method-level FL (\code{methFL}), the performance decreases
significantly, indicating \code{methFL}'s contribution.
